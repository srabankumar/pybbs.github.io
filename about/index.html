About

Started in 2013, PyBBS, the Bhubaneswar Region Python Group, is focused on three goals:

  • Teach people about Python
  • Advocate for the use of Python
  • Provide a platform for people interested in python and programming to meet each other and discuss their interest.

Google Group

Our main point of contact is a Google group. We plan, discuss and announce our upcoming events there. Please join the group if you want to attend or help in organizing events.

IRC

Our IRC channel is #pybbs on the freenode network. Some of us hang out there occasionally. Come join us if you want to have a chat.
